About

Hi! I'm a passionate Cybersecurity enthusiast and a 3rd-year B.Tech Computer Science student with a specialization in Cybersecurity. I thrive on solving complex problems and exploring the ever-evolving world of digital security.
My journey into tech started with a curiosity about how systems work — and more importantly, how they can be broken and protected. Over time, I've built hands-on experience through personal projects, internships, and platforms like TryHackMe.
